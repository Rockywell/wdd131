Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of our Solar System. It powers life on Earth through its light and heat.

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet, with extreme temperature fluctuations and a cratered surface.

Venus

Venus is similar in size to Earth but has a thick, toxic atmosphere and surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.

Earth

Earth is our home planet, the only one known to harbor life, with abundant water and a protective atmosphere.

Mars

Mars is the Red Planet, known for its iron oxide surface, dusty landscape, and two small moons.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet, a gas giant with a Great Red Spot and dozens of moons.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning rings, composed of ice and rock, and a host of diverse moons.

Uranus

Uranus is an ice giant with a tilted rotation axis, giving it extreme seasons.

Neptune

Neptune is an ice giant known for strong winds, deep blue color, and its moon Triton.
